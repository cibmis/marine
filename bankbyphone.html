Keeping You Connected with Ready Line - 800-423-0806

Ready Line is Central Illinois Bank's 24-hour automated telephone information service designed to keep you connected with secure access to your Checking, NOW, Savings, Planned Savings, Money Market, CD's, IRA's and Loan accounts any time, day or night, seven days a week.

Ready Line Application

Ready Line Business Application

Activation is easy. First, have your account number and Social security number or Tax Identification number that is listed on the account available; then dial (800) 423-0806 from a touch-tone phone. You will be prompted to establish a Personal Identification Number (PIN). After activation, your account number and (PIN) is all you will need to get easy access to:

  • Account balance information
  • Account history for the previous 6 months of account activity
  • Specific check information
  • Current and previous YTD interest
  • Loan inquiries
  • CD inquiries
  • Funds Transfer access*

If you have any questions or need assistance with Ready Line, please contact a Branch Manager or Personal Banker at your local branch.

*Transfer Funds - In order to perform a funds transfer, we require an application to be completed to specify the accounts you request to transfer to/from. Please contact a Branch Manager or Personal Banker for an application. Transfers requested after 6:00 p.m. Central Time will be posted on the next business day. There is no limit on the amount a customer can transfer, the current balance will be used to determine Funds Transfer acceptance.
