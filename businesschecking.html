Different sized businesses have different needs. We offer three different business accounts, so that you can choose the one that is perfect for your business. Your local banker can help you every step of the way.

Basic Business Checking

Our Basic Business Checking account is an easy to understand account designed for businesses with low transaction counts.

  • Minimum opening deposit: $100
  • Minimum balance: $5,000
  • Monthly service charge: $7.50 (if minimum balance falls below $5,000)
  • First 75 items included, additional $0.25 each thereafter
  • Online banking (1 user) with bill pay and eStatements
  • Mobile banking
  • Deposit Anywhere (subject to approval, see local branch for more information)

Advantage Business Checking

The Advantage Business Checking account is designed for mid-sized businesses with simple cash management needs.

  • Minimum opening deposit: $100
  • Minimum balance: $25,000
  • Monthly service charge: $20 (if minimum balance falls below $25,000)
  • First 250 items included, additional $.25 each thereafter
  • Online banking (up to 2 users) with bill pay and eStatements
  • Mobile banking
  • Deposit Anywhere (subject to approval, see local branch for more information)

Premier Business Checking

Our Premier Business Checking is an analysis style account. It is customizable to fit your needs and the monthly service charge can be offset with an earnings credit.

  • Minimum opening deposit: $100
  • Treasury management services customized to suit your needs
  • An earnings credit rate (ECR) is applied which may offset or eliminate service charge fees
  • Next day availability on most items
  • Itemized statements are available, detailing monthly transactions
  • Various statement options available including eStatements
