Coverdell Education Savings Account

The CESA account is designed to help parents and students save for educational expenses. Amounts in the account grow tax free until distributed. Annual contributions are nondeductible and limited to $2,000 per beneficiary. They can only be made until the account holder or designated beneficiary reaches age 18. Visit your local branch for more information.

*An early closing fee in the amount of $25.00 may be debited from the account if account is closed prior to six (6) months.
