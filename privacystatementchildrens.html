Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

About Children’s Online Privacy

The Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) was passed to give parents increased control over what information is collected from their children online and how such information is used. The law applies to websites and services directed to, and which knowingly collect information from, children under the age of 13. CIBM Bank’s Web sites and online services are not directed to children under the age of 13, nor is information knowingly collected from them.
