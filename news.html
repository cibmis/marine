Equifax Data Breach
September 8, 2017

Equifax, one of the three major credit reporting agencies, recently announced that its computer systems had been breached by hackers earlier this summer.

Records of approximately 143 million U.S. consumers have been compromised, including names, social security numbers, birth dates, addresses, and in some cases, drivers' license numbers. Additionally, credit card numbers for over 200,000 U.S. consumers were also accessed.

Equifax has created a website with information regarding this breach: https://www.equifaxsecurity2017.com. Please read and understand the terms of the credit monitoring services being offered by Equifax before registering.

The FTC has also provided additional information on "What to Do" which can be found here: https://www.consumer.ftc.gov/blog/2017/09/equifax-data-breach-what-do.

Tax Forms are now available through Online Banking
November 29, 2016

Follow these 6 steps to enroll.

  1. Login to Online Banking
  2. Select the eStatements tab
  3. Select Sign Up/Changes
  4. Open the “+” next to each account enrolled to see available document types
  5. Check the box next to the tax form you wish to receive electronically
  6. Select Save Settings at the bottom of the screen
